PLANT NITE®

Don’t Miss This Unbe-leaf-able Event

Plan your Plant Nite®! Change up your Friday night plans and build a terrarium at the Aquarium. Create a step-by-step unique plant masterpiece, led by an OH-FISH-AL Plant Nite® instructor, while sipping on a drink (alcoholic & non-alcoholic) from our cash bar. Swim by before 7 PM to enjoy the underwater world of Ripley’s Aquarium – or stick around after your session!

Your $60 ticket (19+) includes:

  • Same-day general admission as your Plant Nite® session.
  • Complimentary ticket to swim by another day!
  • Your beautiful terrarium to take home.

*Please note that your terrarium design will not be exactly as pictured.
