PAINT NITE®

Paint Nite® at the Aquarium (19+ event!)

Embrace your colours and let your creativity flow in a vibrant underwater oasis! Join us for a Paint Nite® session at the Aquarium located right in the heart of downtown Toronto.

Led by an O-FISH-AL Paint Nite® instructor, create a marine-themed canvas painting while you sip on a drink! Need some aquatic inspiration? Swim on by before the 6:00 p.m. start time and enjoy the colourful views of the underwater world.

Your $50 ticket (19+) includes:

  • Same-day general admission as your Paint Nite® session.
  • Complimentary ticket to swim by another day!
  • Your beautiful 16×20 canvas to take home.

*Tickets for this event are non-refundable.
*Beverages and snacks are available at an additional cost (debit and credit only).
